This is a novel about pursuing dreams, telling the fantastical adventure of Santiago, a Spanish shepherd boy.

The core idea that runs through the book is: "When you truly desire something, the entire universe conspires to help you achieve it."

This book is simple yet profound. Through a simple fable, it explores the meaning of life, dreams, and destiny. It tells us to bravely follow our hearts and not be bound by fear and comfort.
